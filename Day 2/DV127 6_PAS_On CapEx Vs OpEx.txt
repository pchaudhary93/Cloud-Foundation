	Q5
	What is CapEx (Capital expenditure)?
	ANS
	It is spending money on physical infrastructure/hardwares. 


	Q6
	What is OpEx (Operational expenditure)?
	ANS
	Operational expenditure is spending money on services or products and being billed for the companies.
